About

Background

Liam Dawson is a developer and photographer living in Brighton.

As a street phographer, his work focuses on humanistic themes and portraiture of everyday life in Brighton and London, drawing inspiration from photographers such as Ernst Haas, Saul Leiter and Fan Ho.

He works in digital mediums and shares work and photographic notes on his Instagram account; @liamrdawson, where he engages with an enthusiastic and passionate group of amateur and professional photographers alike.
