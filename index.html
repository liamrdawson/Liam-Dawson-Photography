Liam Dawson is a Welsh photographer now living in Brighton. His work focuses on candid street photography and portraiture of the people who live in Brighton and nearby London. This website is a collection of his best work, as well as sources of inspiration.

“I am not interested in shooting new things ― I am interested to see things new.”
